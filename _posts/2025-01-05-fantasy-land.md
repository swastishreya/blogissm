Quietly sobbing away \
On a journey back \
To my fantasy land \
Forced to endure \
The fact that time \
Was indeed fleeting \
Truth never certain \
Hopelessly tugging \
At the fabric of memories \
Life nothing but futile \
An attempt to gloss over \
The pain of being \
By an ecstasy of happiness.
